Combine 2 Spotify Playlists

Here's a Web App that takes the sound qualities of 2 spofity playlists to create a new playlist that is most-similar to both playlists. I got the idea to build this when my roommate and his girlfriend couldn't decide what to watch on Netflix and thought it'd be cool to build something that recommended a movie they'd both like. But Netflix doesn't have a public API, and Spotify does. So here's that same idea but for Spotify.
